Chief Data Office

Empowering the U.S. Air Force with trusted data, resilient digital architecture, and decision advantage at every level of command.

Office Mission

The Chief Data Office leads enterprise data strategy across the Air Force to improve readiness, modernize operations, and accelerate mission outcomes. We align governance, analytics, and AI-enabled capabilities so Airmen and Guardians can act faster with confidence.

Prepared Speeches for Air Force Events

Speech 1: Data as a Warfighting Capability

Good morning team. Today, we recognize that data is no longer a back-office resource. It is a core warfighting capability. Every sortie flown, every maintenance action completed, and every mission decision made improves when data is visible, reliable, and actionable. Our responsibility is to ensure that information moves as fast as operations. We will continue building secure data pathways, strengthening digital skills, and delivering tools that place insight directly in the hands of our Airmen.

Speech 2: Building Trust Through Governance

Thank you for your dedication to the mission. Effective governance is not about slowing innovation; it is about enabling trust at scale. When we define standards, protect data integrity, and establish clear ownership, we create the conditions for faster decision-making. In the Chief Data Office, we are committed to transparent stewardship so commanders can rely on one shared picture of operations. Trust in data means trust in outcomes, and trust in outcomes means mission success.

Speech 3: The Future Force and AI Readiness

As we prepare for tomorrow's challenges, we must integrate AI and advanced analytics responsibly and decisively. Technology alone does not win; prepared people do. Our focus is on equipping the force with practical tools, repeatable training, and ethical guardrails that keep us mission-focused and accountable. The Air Force has always transformed to meet emerging threats. Through disciplined data strategy, we will continue to lead from the front and secure advantage for the nation.
